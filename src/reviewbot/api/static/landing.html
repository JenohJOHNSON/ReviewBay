// The pipeline

One pipeline. From scattered reviews to sourced answers.

Four stages that run themselves: collect every public mention, store it, give it meaning, and put an analyst on top of it.

/ Scrape

Every public mention. Collected on schedule.

Collectors watch review sites, social platforms, app stores, and forums for your brand. New mentions land in the pipeline within minutes, deduplicated, timestamped, and source-linked.

7 source families · Incremental crawls · Original URL kept
/ Store

Everything lands in Neon and pgvector. One schema.

Raw mentions are normalized into a single review model: source, author, rating, sentiment, product line. Your Postgres, your data, queryable from day one.

Neon Postgres · ELT, not CSVs · Full history retained
/ Embed

OpenAI embeddings turn text into meaning.

Every review is embedded into a 768-dimensional vector and searched with pgvector. Semantic search finds the complaint about "checkout freezing" even when nobody used those words.

OpenAI embeddings · pgvector search · Semantic retrieval
/ Ask

An AI analyst with receipts.

Ask in plain English. The AI answers from retrieved reviews only, and every claim links back to the original post. No hallucination. No black box.

RAG over your reviews · Cited answers · Plain-English queries
