Questions, answered

Where do the reviews come from?

The open web, the Apple App Store, and Google Play. Each source has its own collector, and the web source can be pointed at a brand's real website so it finds the right company instead of a lookalike.

Do I need to set anything up?

No. Add a brand name and, if you have it, a website. Collection starts on its own and the dashboard fills in as reviews arrive.

Where does the chat get its answers?

Only from the reviews ReviewBay has collected. It does not guess from general knowledge, and every claim links back to the specific review it came from, so you can check it yourself.

Will other people see the brands I test?

No. The list of brands you add is remembered in your browser only, not on a shared server. If someone else opens ReviewBay on their own device, they start with a clean slate. (Full accounts are on the way.)

How fresh is the data?

Reviews refresh on a regular schedule in the background, so what you see keeps up with what customers are saying.
